Dirk Deam is running a campaign based on building community across the 39 counties of Iowa's 4th District. His campaign will be focused primarily on local outreach and creating conversations across partisan lines.

Contributions or gifts to Dirk Deam's Exploratory Campaign are not tax deductible. Federal law requires our campaign to use our best efforts to collect and report the name, mailing address, occupation, and name of employer of contributors whose contributions exceed $200 per election cycle. The campaign cannot accept cash contributions in excess of $100 per person. No person may contribute in excess of $2700 per person per election (primary and general are separate). In addition, we cannot accept any donations from corporations, labor organizations, national banks, foreign nationals, LLCs, or partnerships.
