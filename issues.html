Congressional Campaign for Iowa's 4th District



Dirk's efforts are focused on building community through conversation across the 39 counties of Iowa's 4th District. By engaging and listening to the people of Iowa, from our small manufacturing cities to our rural farming towns, Dirk hopes to engage in common sense politics. Iowa has a long progressive history, based on face-to-face interaction and recognition of our common values. Recently, ideology and partisanship have driven us apart. We all want better. Our goal of a more inclusive and optimistic politics will take hard work--lucky for us all, that's one of Iowa's dearest values.
